Retro Game Music Pack by Feliks Balzer (Pizzamakesgames)

The Retro Game Music Pack contains a variety of 8Bit & 16Bit Chiptune music, wonderfully suited for retro games of any kind.

In total, this pack includes:
- 10 16Bit Loops
- 10 8Bit Loops
- 5 Gameboy Loops
- 5 C64 Loops

All tracks loop seamlessly and are added to your project by simply dragging the song you want to use into your hierarchy window.
